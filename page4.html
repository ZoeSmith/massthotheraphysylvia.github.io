About Sylvia

Welcome to Massotherapy Sylvia where you are invited to come enjoy a massage and reset your inner compass. 


Sylvia graduated from Humber College with 3 certificates one in equestrian skills, one in equestrian coaching and one in small business management. She used these degrees to teach horse back riding lessons.
From there, Sylvia changed her career path and focused on domestic aid and cleaning for senior citizens and those in need of a little extra help while also volunteering her time at the schools her children attended.
Now Sylvia has found her new career path in massage therapy after graduating from Massotherapy Adam she now is helping people to feel and be their best.
Her goal is to help you relax, no matter if that is from stress or muscle pain.
